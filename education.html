My education began on kindergarten. When i was 5 years old. After kindergarten, I entered pre-school when I was 6 years old. When I was in grade 1, I went to primary school at Sk Kamil 3. Then I moved to Sk Kampung Nara which is near my house. Here, I enter many sports activities and other programs. I sat for the UPSR here and passed the exam well.

Throughout my high school years, my educational experience had its ups and downs. My first high school is SMA Tengku Amalin Ai'shah Putri. All the students there are female students. I love studying there. I love that school. But when I was in form 2, I had to move because I lived in a hostel. This school is far from my home. But, I need to go to tuition classes. Then I moved to SMK Seri Aman school where my mom teaches there. Since my mom teach there, many teachers and students knew me. I have entered a lot of activities. I am a school prefect. I studied here until I finished SPM and STPM with excellent results. There are many memories that I cannot forget with my friends there. I miss my high school.

Now, I am in semester 2 in the Bachelor's degree in Office Management at UITM Kelantan. I started studying here by staying in college during semester 1. In college, I met a lot of good friends. Now, I live in a rented house with my classmates. Now, we are getting closer. We both want to chase the dean. I hope we success soon.
